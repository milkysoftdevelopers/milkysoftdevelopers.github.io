Description

Want to listen song? Try YouPlay!

You can use this app to listen YouTube videos songs.

This application can helps you to listen your favorite songs while you are working on another application or your device screen is off. To do this you will need to search the name of song in search box and then select that song from the list and play it enjoy....

If you want to listen songs while you want to work on another app so need to play song and then press home button and do your stuff while song is playing in background but if you want to listen songs while your device screen is off so you will need to press power button once after playing song enjoy....

If you want to see full video of song so you will need to open 3 dots menu on top right corner and ten tap on 'Open in Youtube' button (and select youtube from list if requires) then your song video will be play on youtube. If you are facing any problem with this app just select support from 3 dots menu or use our support link.
