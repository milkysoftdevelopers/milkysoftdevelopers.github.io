YouPlay Privacy Policies

This application YouPlay : Listen your favorite songs helps you to listen your favorite songs while you are working or your device screen is off.
This application require google account sign in to improve users experience but you can skip sign in and continue listen your favorite songs without sign in.
This application plays youtube videos in background of your device. So you can do your stuff while your favorite songs are playing in background.
This application not allows users to download videos from Youtube.

If you need more information about YouPlay please contact us for YouPlay at: youplay.milkysoft@gmail.com

YouPlay Permissions

1: Uses Permission: (Internet): This permission is required to keep you connected with the online world and to do some online activities.

2: Uses Permission: (Read Phone State): This permission is required to play/pause music on incoming/outgoing calls.
